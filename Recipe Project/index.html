Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Milk Powder
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Mix milk powder, flour, and butter
  2. Roll dough into small balls and fry golden brown.
  3. Boil sugar syrup with cardamom until thick.
  4. Soak fried balls in syrup, let them absorb, and serve.
